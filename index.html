About US

At Charlee's Bee company we have our family involved in the beekeepng operations.We started this out in 2000,with few heives,and today company of polinators have increased with addin of many heives ,& supply of bee products.We have a big colony set up for the bee families at Ashville ,North Carolina.Here we try our best to balance the nature and supply its goodness through our products like wax,honey,propolis & pollen grains.

Our Honey

All our efforts are to maintain high quality standards in processing the bee products.Our honey is different from the honey that you get at supermarkets,because here the honey is only heated enough to pump and strain.The result is a full-bodied taste, preserving all of the natural and healthful properties of natural sweetener.

Bee Wax

Beeswax is a natural wax produced by honey bees of the Genus Apis.This wax is produced by 8 glands in abdomenial segments of worker bees, who discard it in or at the hive. This bee wax has good medicainal and healing properties that is why it is used in many skin products like soap,lotions & lipbalms.
